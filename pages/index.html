Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Information Technology

Prepare for a fast-paced career designing, building, and securing modern information systems. Our B.Tech in IT blends software engineering, networking, cybersecurity, data systems, and project-based learning to produce industry-ready graduates.

Core Program Tracks

Software Engineering

Admission & How to Apply

Eligibility: High school diploma with strong mathematics; specific country/institution requirements may apply. International students should check visa and language requirements.

  1. Prepare transcripts, CV, and a statement of purpose.
  2. Submit the online application and pay the application fee.
  3. Attend interview (if required) and provide references.
  4. Receive offer and confirm enrollment before the deadline.

Scholarships: Merit- and need-based scholarships are available — check the scholarships page for details.
